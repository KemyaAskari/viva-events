Who we are

Viva Events is an event planning and decoration business based in Sydney NSW. We help customers with event design, styling, decoration, setup and event-day presentation.

Our goal is to make the booking process simple and clear, so customers understand their package, inclusions, setup needs, pricing and responsibilities before the event day.

Our values

  • Clear and respectful communication
  • Professional event setup and presentation
  • Safe, clean and organised styling
  • Respect for venue rules and access times
  • Honest pricing, inclusions and booking information
