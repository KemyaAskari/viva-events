What customers should send us

To prepare the best quote, please send as much information as possible. This helps us recommend the right package and avoid confusion later.

  • Event type and event date
  • Venue name, suburb and address
  • Guest number and event time
  • Theme, colours and inspiration photos
  • Preferred package or budget range
  • Venue access time and pack-down time
  • Parking, lift access, stairs or loading information
  • Power access if lighting or signs are needed
  • Any venue rules about candles, balloons, confetti or hanging décor

What affects the final quote?

Final pricing depends on the event size, styling level and venue requirements.

  • Event location and travel distance
  • Number of guests and venue size
  • Complexity of theme and decoration
  • Custom flowers, signage or props
  • Setup and pack-down time
  • Last-minute changes or extra requests
  • Venue restrictions or difficult access
