Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Viva Events collects, uses and protects your personal information.

This policy is written in plain English and complies with Australian privacy laws.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

Viva Events (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling personal information responsibly and transparently.

We comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Event details (date, venue, event type, guest numbers, budget)
  • Styling preferences and inspiration
  • Messages or enquiries you send to us

Providing personal information is optional, however it may be required for us to provide quotes or services.

3. How We Collect Information

  • Website enquiry and contact forms
  • Email, phone or social media communication
  • Quotes, bookings and consultations

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and prepare quotes
  • Plan, manage and deliver event styling services
  • Communicate booking details or changes
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Meet legal and administrative obligations

5. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information.

Information may be shared only:

  • With venues or suppliers involved in your event
  • Where required by law or legal process
  • To protect our legal rights or prevent fraud

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

7. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to improve performance and understand visitor behaviour. These tools do not personally identify you.

8. Marketing & Spam Compliance

We comply with the Spam Act 2003. We only send communications related to your enquiry or booking, unless you have given consent for marketing.

9. Access & Correction

You may request access to your personal information or request corrections if information is inaccurate or outdated.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms such as social media. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

11. Consumer Rights

Nothing in this policy limits your rights under the Australian Consumer Law (ACL).

12. Copyright

All website content, images and designs are protected under the Copyright Act 1968.

13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page.
